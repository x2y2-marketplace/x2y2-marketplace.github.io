What is X2Y2 Exchange?

X2Y2 is an exchange decentralized that handles yield farming and token swaps via the use of an Automated Market Maker (AMM) smart contract. Based on Ethereum this exchange is a more advanced version of the X2Y2 Protocol as well as a second DEX using the same AMM system.


The exchange financially rewards liquidity providers to provide tokens onto the exchange by providing them with fees. Particularly, they receive the cost that other traders have to pay for token swaps.


To provide price changes, X2Y2 utilizes a number of decentralized oracles. The list includes X2Y2's TWAP oracle, Compound's open Price feed, as well as oracles developed by Chainlink. In addition, arbitrageurs help in delivering exact prices' by reducing the price variances on every trading pair.
